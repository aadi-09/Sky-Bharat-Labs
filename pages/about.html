About Us

Sky Bharat Labs is dedicated to bridging the gap in emerging technologies by providing STEAM-based education to both urban and rural communities. Our mission is to empower students and professionals with cutting-edge knowledge that extends beyond traditional curricula.

What We Do

We specialize in training and innovation in the following fields:

Aeromodelling
Drone Technology
Robotics & Mechatronics
3D Designing & Modelling
Advanced Computing
Renewable Energy
AI & Machine Learning
Space Technology

Beyond technology, we encourage holistic development through competitions and creative activities such as sports, singing, and art.
